About LAMS - Leave & Attendance Management System!


The Leave and Attendance Management system (LAMS) has following main components:

Attendance/Leave Section:
Leave and Attendance Management system will allow user to apply for different type of leaves (Paid leave/Optional leave/Leave without Pay/Maternity leave/Paternity leave/Child adoption leave), view leave transactions, view reportee's leave applications. This section will also allow user to view the attendance records for past 2 weeks including current week status. Employees will be allowed to apply for optional holiday from Attendance/leave section.

Actions
This section allows the user to approve/reject reportee's leave/attendance regularization. This section will also allow the employee to apply for leave/attendance regularization for his direct/indirect reportees.

HR Management Section:
This section is available to HR Managers only, facilitates them to manage leave years, holiday information, employee's information on PSI Deputation, special leave approval and pending leave approval.

Reports:
Various reports like leave transaction details, leave summary and leave balance can be generated depending upon the role of the user who has logged in.

Help:
Help includes a set of FAQ and a presentation on the usage of leave and attendance management system and also a link to the employee manual.
